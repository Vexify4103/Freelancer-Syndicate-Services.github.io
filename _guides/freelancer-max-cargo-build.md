## Why the Freelancer MAX?

The **MISC Freelancer MAX** is one of the best cargo ships in the game for solo and small-crew hauling. With 120 SCU of cargo space and solid maneuverability for its size, it's the backbone of many FSS trading operations.

## Recommended Loadout

### Power Plant
- **Juno Starwerk WhiteOut** (Size 2) — efficient, stable output

### Coolers
- **Juno Starwerk Snow Blind x2** (Size 1) — keeps temps down on long hauls

### Shields
- **Behr Defender x2** (Size 2) — reliable all-purpose shields

### Weapons (2× Size 3 wing mounts)
- **Klaus & Werner CF-337 Panther Repeaters** — good all-around defense against pirates
- Alternatively leave them light if you're running in safe space

### Missiles
- **Talon Stalker x8** on the wing racks — fire-and-forget deterrent

## Flight Tips

- **Quantum fuel:** Always start full. The MAX has a medium-range Q-drive — plan your fuel stops on long routes.
- **Landing pads:** The MAX is a large ship — it requires a medium or large pad. Confirm pad availability before heading to small outposts.
- **Shields up, always.** Cargo ships are prime pirate targets. Keep shields charged and weapons ready even in "safe" space.

## Top Cargo Routes (Current Patch)

These routes change with patches — check [sc-trade.tools](https://sc-trade.tools) for current pricing:

1. **Microtech → Crusader** — Agricultural supplies
2. **ArcCorp → Microtech** — Medical supplies
3. **Hurston → CRU-L5** — Industrial materials

## Upgrading to the MOLE

Once you've saved up from Freelancer MAX runs (~3–5M aUEC), consider upgrading to a **MOLE** for group mining ops. Higher upfront cost but the yield with a 3-person crew is unmatched.
