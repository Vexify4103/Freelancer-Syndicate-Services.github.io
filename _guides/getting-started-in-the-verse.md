## Welcome to the 'Verse

Star Citizen can be overwhelming at first. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to survive — and thrive — in your first sessions.

## Step 1: Create Your Character

After launching the game, you'll be prompted to create your citizen. Choose your starting location (Microtech, ArcCorp, or Hurston are all good starting points) and customize your appearance.

**Tip:** Your starting location doesn't lock you in. You can travel anywhere once you're in-game.

## Step 2: Learn the Basic Controls

Before heading out, spend 15–20 minutes in the tutorial missions. Key controls to learn:

| Action | Default Key |
|--------|-------------|
| Exit seat | `Y` (hold) |
| Open MobiGlas | `F1` |
| Open inventory | `I` |
| Quantum travel | `B` |
| Request landing | `V` |

## Step 3: Your First Ship

You'll start with a loaner ship appropriate to your game package. If you have a **Freelancer**, you're well set for cargo runs. If you have a **Aurora** or **Mustang**, start with local delivery missions to build up credits.

## Step 4: Your First Mission

1. Open your **MobiGlas** (`F1`)
2. Navigate to the **Contracts** app
3. Look for **Delivery** missions — they're the safest for new players
4. Pick up your package from the marker and deliver it

You'll earn a small amount of aUEC (in-game currency) for each delivery. Use this to buy supplies and eventually upgrade your ship.

## Step 5: Join Org Operations

Once you're comfortable with basic movement and missions, jump into an org operation! Post in our Discord when you're online — there's almost always someone to fly with.
