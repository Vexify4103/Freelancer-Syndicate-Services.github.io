Enlist Today

Join the Syndicate

We're always looking for capable pilots, traders, miners, and fighters to grow our ranks. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or just starting out in the 'verse — there's a place for you here.

Join our Discord Apply on RSI

What We Look For

  • Willingness to cooperate and contribute to the org
  • Respectful conduct toward fellow members and others
  • Activity in the community — Discord, missions, org events
  • Any playstyle: combat, trade, mining, exploration — all welcome
  • New players are fully welcome — we'll help you get started

What You Get

  • An active crew to fly with across the 'verse
  • Access to our full guide library for every playstyle
  • Org events: group mining ops, convoy runs, PvP fleets
  • Mentorship from experienced pilots and traders
  • A welcoming community of fellow Star Citizen fans

How to Join

01

Join our Discord

Head to our Discord server using the link above. Introduce yourself in #introductions and say hi to the crew.

02

Apply on RSI

Visit our official RSI organization page and click "Apply." Make sure to mention your Discord handle.

03

Get accepted

An officer will review your application and approve it — typically within 24–48 hours. We'll welcome you in Discord too.

04

Start flying

Check our guide library, find some org mates online, and get into the 'verse.
